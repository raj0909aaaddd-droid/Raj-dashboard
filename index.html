The Destined One
You awaken deep in the Black Wind Mountain. You travel through the dense, rotting Bamboo Grove. Defeating lesser wolf demons, you find an ancient shrine.
Wise Old Man (Yuan Shoucheng)
An eccentric elder appears, pointing to the old gourd on your belt. "Ah, a Destined One! To fight Yaoguai, you cannot just hit them. You must absorb their essence."
SHHHING!

The Narrator
You travel further, finding a clearing engulfed in flames. Guangzhi, the Fire Wolf, spins his blazing twin-blades. After a brutal fight, you strike him down and absorb his spirit into your newly upgraded gourd.
Behind his arena, you find it. A massive, ancient bronze bell hanging from a dead tree.
DOOOOOONG.
The Cutscene Vision
The sound warps reality. You see a vision of the past: A giant wolf hanging from a noose, a temple burning, and the tragic screams of monks. The mystery deepens.
You trek deeper into the Snake Trail. The earth shakes. The Wandering Wight—a corrupted monk with a massive bronze head—blocks the path. You dodge his earth-shattering palms, strike him down, and absorb his iron-hard essence.
In the cave behind him, you find the second bell. You strike it with your staff.
DOOOOOONG.
The Cutscene Vision
Another flash. You see a golden cassock glowing in the dark, surrounded by greedy, blood-soaked hands. Someone murdered for that robe.
At the Guanyin Temple ruins, you slay the giant White Wolf, Lingxuzi. Behind the temple's main altar, hidden in the shadows, hangs the final ancient bell.
DOOOOOONG.
The Narrator
Three bells rung. The sky turns completely white. The ruins around you rebuild themselves in reverse. You are pulled through a rift in time, arriving in a secret area: The Ancient Guanyin Temple.
The Narrator
The secret temple is pristine, but the floor is overflowing with cursed gold. In the center floats a grotesque, giant monk with an impossibly bloated head, draped in golden robes. Elder Jinchi. The monk whose greed burned the temple centuries ago.
Elder Jinchi
"THE CASSOCK! I BURNED THEM ALL FOR THE CASSOCK! IT IS MINE!"
WAAAAASHHH!
He summons waves of explosive zombie monks. You transform into the Fire Wolf, slicing through the undead horde. You reach Jinchi, shattering his golden shield and putting his greedy, cursed soul to rest forever. You claim the Fireproof Mantle as your reward.
You return to the present and scale to the peak of Black Wind Mountain. The Black Bear Guai awaits. "You killed my monks. You broke my gates!" he roars, transforming into a tornado of black wind.
But with the Fireproof Mantle protecting you, his flames cannot burn your fur. You cast Immobilize, bringing the Jingu Bang down on his skull with the force of a falling star. The King falls.
